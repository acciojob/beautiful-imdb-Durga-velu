This movie name is Ponniyin Selvan. It is true story based historical movie. ponniyin selvan is alternate fiction by written kalki. Also known as Rajaraja Cholan, Arulmozhi is called Ponniyin Selvan because he once fell into the Cauvery river, also called Ponni Nadhi, and was apparently "saved" by the river goddess. So, he became Ponniyin Selvan, meaning "son of river Ponni".
